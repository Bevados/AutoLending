About us

Allamuchy Auto Parts is the leading auto parts recycling facility in Northern NJ serving the New York Tri-State area. With over 30 years of experience in the auto industry, we strive to bring you quality auto parts for a fraction of the price.

Serving both auto repair facilities and the general public, we offer the highest demanded late model auto parts as well as outsourcing services to bring you the best of the best on the local market.

Why shop used parts
